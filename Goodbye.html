Goodbye



Hello,

If you’re reading this I am dead or almost there.
If you’re not mentioned its because you didn’t know me well, at least in the end.
Please make sure my mum and dad are okay.
I want mum to move on, achieve her dreams and be happy.
Dad’s already lost two kids and I need you all to make sure he’s okay.

You may be wondering why I chose to end my life.
I no longer wish to live for other people.
I lived with depression for around ten years, suicidal thoughts for 6 - almost 7.
The only thing I wanted, truly, was for things to get better but they never did.
I didn’t want to end it for so long, just because I didn’t want you all to suffer.
I can’t go on anymore.

I started running when I was 15, got good at it, then injured my legs.
My brain could do the academic stuff well, at least until COVID, now thoughts and words are just jumbled.
I lost my confidence and drive, because every part of me seemed broken or incomplete.

I never kept friends long, they just seemed to forget about or ignore me in the end.
I think I became easier to be around as I grew up.
This didn’t matter much as things just turned out the same way.
I’ve always felt a sense of loneliness, that couldn’t be quelled no matter what I tried.
The feelings of otherness and a sense of disquiet seemed to grow worse over time.
I did’t think I would ever have somebody who stays or I enjoy being around enough to make forget how I felt.

I wasn’t a part of my family, at least not really. I always felt like I was the last to be remembered, last to know.
One of the toughest feeling is feeling like an outsider around your flesh and blood.
It just felt there was a gap between me and them, that would never close.
Deep down, I didn’t hate any of you, I just wish things could’ve been better.
Feel free to keep any of my clothes, books or films.
Sell the music stuff though, it’s pretty valuable.
Use the money to cremate me and use whatever’s left over on something nice.
I didn’t die with any debts, so there’s nothing to worry about there.


Goodbye, Romario
